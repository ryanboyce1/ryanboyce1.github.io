The League of Women Voters

Student Assignment: Engage users inside and outside the digital realm

We were given the challenge to improve The League's digital properties. This would in turn directly impact it's ability to fulfill it's mission of education and engagment

The scope of this project was two weeks. To get all the research we needed to present a case study, we had to think quickly.

  • Step 1

    Strategy

    Before we met with The League of Women Voters leadership, we wanted to research and imagine who the users were, and who the target users could be. Empathy maps and proto-personas helped us put ourselves in the shoes of the target audience.

  • Step 2

    Stakeholder Meetings

    We scheduled time to meet with the leaders of TLWV to talk about possible pain points in membership. We wanted to know who were their current users, who were their targeted users and for them to give us insite to their current digital platform

  • Step 3

    Research and Analysis

    The bulk of our project included research. We conducted several interviews and surveys to uncover what experience people of all ages have in regards to politcal engement. We found here our challenge was larger than just improving a digital platform. We became advocates for TLWV in helping them understand how to better reach out to the community, and how to keep people engaged in the political process.

  • Step 4

    Non-Digital Engagement

    From what we gathered, TLWV has a tough time engaging younger voters at all let alone the few that would use their web platform. We encouraged them to reach out in several different ways that have helped other comparitive organizations gain traction in their cause
